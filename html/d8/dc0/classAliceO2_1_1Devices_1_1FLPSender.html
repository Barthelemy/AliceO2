Detailed Description

Sends sub-timframes to epnReceivers

Sub-timeframes are received from the previous step (or generated in test-mode) and are sent to epnReceivers. Target epnReceiver is determined from the timeframe ID: targetEpnReceiver = timeframeId % numEPNs (numEPNs is same for every flpSender, although some may be inactive).
